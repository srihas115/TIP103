[*CodePath's Code of Conduct*](https://www.codepath.org/code-of-conduct_students)

### [](https://courses.codepath.org/courses/tip103/pages/getting_started#heading-be-present)🎒 Be present

We’re so glad you’re here! Attendance is expected at all sessions.

- ✅ Up to **3 absences** allowed (no request needed).
- ✅ Attendance is checked throughout class; missing key portions may count as an absence.
- ✅ Coursework is still due on time, even if you’re absent.

### [](https://courses.codepath.org/courses/tip103/pages/getting_started#heading-actively-participate)🙋 Actively participate

This is a highly interactive course. To remain enrolled, students must:

- ✅ Be on camera and in frame from the shoulders up with an appropriate background (Zoom backgrounds are okay! Zoom avatars are not).
- ✅ Log in from a laptop or tablet (phones and devices you can’t actively code with are not allowed).
- ✅ Have a stable internet connection.
- ✅ Be in a quiet space and participate fully—no traveling, working, or multitasking.

⚠️ Not meeting these guidelines may result in absences or removal from the course.

### [](https://courses.codepath.org/courses/tip103/pages/getting_started#heading-coursework)📖 Coursework

- ✅ Submit one HackerRank assessment from any unit**by Sunday at 11:59 PM PT**.
- ✅ If you miss the deadline, a**48-hour extension**is automatically available. No request needed.
- ✅ All coursework must be submitted within 48-hours of the deadline to stay enrolled.

### [](https://courses.codepath.org/courses/tip103/pages/getting_started#heading-course-status-changes)🚨 Course Status Changes

You may be moved to observer status if:

- ❌ You exceed **3 absences** (including partial participation, such as being off-camera or traveling).
- ❌ You miss **1 assignment** (by not submitting within 48-hours of the deadline).

### [](https://courses.codepath.org/courses/tip103/pages/getting_started#heading-observer-status)👀 Observer Status

Observers:

- 🔹 Retain access to [course materials](https://courses.codepath.org/) and recordings.
- ❌ Do not receive a CodePath certificate.

### [](https://courses.codepath.org/courses/tip103/pages/getting_started#heading-course-completion-alumni-status)✅ Course Completion & Alumni Status

To successfully complete the course and gain alumni status, you must:

- ✔ Meet attendance, participation, and coursework requirements.
- ✔ Pass at least**6 units**by the final day of class.

Graduates will:

- 🏅 Receive a digital certificate.
- 🔗 Join the CodePath alumni network.
